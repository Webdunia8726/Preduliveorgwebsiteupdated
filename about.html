About Us

Empowering Youth and Startups through Innovation, Awareness Education and Employment.

Who We Are

Predulive Edutech Foundation is dedicated to empowering youth and startups by inspiring, raising awareness, providing education and creating employment opportunities. We focus on making a meaningful impact, especially in rural areas, fostering innovation and skill development for a brighter future.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire, aware, educate and employ the youth and startups, driving innovation and creating opportunities for growth. We strive to bridge the gap between education and employment, ensuring sustainable development and progress in underserved communities.

Our Vision

To create a world where every young individual has the opportunity to learn, innovate and thrive. We envision a future where education, employment and entrepreneurship are accessible to all, driving sustainable development and social progress.

Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) Covered

Quality Education (SDG 4)

Ensuring inclusive and equitable quality education and promoting lifelong learning opportunities for all.

Decent Work and Economic Growth (SDG 8)

Promoting sustained, inclusive, and sustainable economic growth, full and productive employment, and decent work for all.

Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure (SDG 9)

Building resilient infrastructure, promoting inclusive and sustainable industrialization, and fostering innovation.

Reduced Inequalities (SDG 10)

Reducing inequality within and among countries, ensuring equal opportunities and reducing inequalities of outcome.

Our Story

2019

Predulive embarked on a mission to empower underprivileged youth, offering them a platform for career guidance and job opportunities.

2020

Despite the challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic, Predulive remained resilient, running social campaigns to provide employment opportunities in small towns and offering drone-based surveillance services to support public safety, recognized by UP Police administrators.

2021

Predulive expanded its impact by partnering with Model Gaon which was mentored by senior IAS officer Dr. Heera Lal who is well known as Dynamic DM for awareness campaigns across numerous villages, earning praise from influential leaders like Shri Amitabh Kant and Sherpa G2

2022

Predulive focused on skill development by organizing technical workshops in engineering colleges, aiming to create a skilled workforce aligned with its vision of empowering communities.

2023

Predulive continued its commitment to education and talent development by offering over 1000 free internships to college students in collaboration with startups, nurturing talent and fostering industry connections.

2024

Finally, Predulive is launching a world 1st integrated platform to support youth from guidance to funding for starting the business, empowering them to pursue entrepreneurial ventures and fostering a culture of innovation in small towns.
